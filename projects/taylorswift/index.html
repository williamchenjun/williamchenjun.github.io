Taylor Swift Song Classification and Reccomendation System

Figures: Taylor Swift's three most recent studio albums, two of which won the "Album of the Year" Grammy.

Project Description

The idea for this project is purely inspired by my liking of Taylor Swift's music. You don't have to like her or her music to acknowledge her impact in the music industry, especially in the last couple of years with "The Eras Tour" and the release of her Grammy-winning album "Midnights".
This project is summarised by the title "Taylor Swift Song Classification and Reccomendation". Thus, technically speaking I am aiming at building two predictive models:

  1. One is for classification. In particular, music attributes classification. This model shall take a song as input and return an array of attributes associated with the song e.g. sad, mellow, energetic, etc..
  2. The second model is going to offer song recommendations based on the attributes the previous model generated.

Therefore, overall, this model is basically going to be a song search engine which matches you with some Taylor Swift songs depending on how you are feeling or some parameters that you pass to it.
