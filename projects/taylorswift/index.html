Taylor Swift Music Analysis and Language Model

Figures: Taylor Swift's three most recent studio albums, two of which won the "Album of the Year" Grammy.

Project Description

This individual project is going to focus on Taylor Swift's discography given her increased popularity and online scrutiny. In particular, we are going to focus on analysing her songwriting and music production. The starting assumptions about her music will be taken from how people perceive it online. The project will be divided into the following sections:

  1. Introduction to Pop Music: Why is it so catchy?
  2. Taylor Swift Involvement in the Pop Genre: From Red to TTPD.
  3. Analysis of Common Themes in Swift's Music: Cluster Analysis
  4. Writing Music in Taylor Swift's Style: A Language Model

Therefore, I want to explore pop music and the reasoning behind its "ear worm" quality. Why are "hooks" the way they are? Perhaps there is a rigorous mathematical reason. Moreover, what I want to mainly explore is Taylor Swift's songwriting and themes. We often stereotype her music as either break up or generic pop music. However, having listened to quite a few of her songs, I believe her success cannot be reduced to just "musical drug" or plain luck.
Once the project is completed, this page will be updated with the final report.
