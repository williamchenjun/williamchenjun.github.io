U.S. Presidential Election Analysis

PDF


Description

This is a group project about the 2016 presidential elections. The paper deals with a statistical analysis of the presidential elections and how the pre-election polls reflected onto the final results. The 2016 election polls are often recalled for how surprising the results were despite the polls majority. In this paper we offer a mathematical and statistical approach to random sampling and polling, and how we could explain the results observed in the 2016 elections.
